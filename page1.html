shaurya OPC cement

Shaurya OPC Cement(शौर्य  ओ पी सी सिमेन्ट ) 

43 grade cement means that the compressive strength of the cement after 28 days is 43 N/mm3 when tested. This grade of cement is used for plain concrete work and plastering works. It is suitable to make concrete mix up to M30. 43 grade cement is also used to make precast items, such as tiles, blocks, pipes, etc. It can be used where setting time is not a necessary concern.

shaurya PSC Cement

Shaurya PSC Cement( शौर्य पी एस सी सिमेन्ट )

PSC stands for Portland Slag cement and Shaurya Cement produces it by either grinding the Portland clinker with gypsum and granulated slag or blending the ground granulated blast furnace slag (GGBS) with Ordinary Portland cement by means of mechanical blenders. Commonly known as a blended cement with the composition of 45-50% slag, 45-50% clinker and 3-5% of gypsum, PSC is a high-performance cement known for its strength, smooth finish
